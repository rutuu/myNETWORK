LinkedIn Connections

How to export from LinkedIn:
  1. Go to LinkedIn → Settings & Privacy
  2. Click Data Privacy → Download your data
  3. Select “Download larger data archive” (includes connections)
  4. Click Request archive — LinkedIn will email you a download link (can take up to 24 hours)
  5. Download the .zip file from that email and unzip it
  6. Find and upload only the Connections.csv file below
⚠ Only upload Connections.csv — other CSV files will not import correctly.
